॥ चिन्मयदीक्षाविधानम् ॥  
  
मल्लिकार्जुनं देवं चिद्घनं छन्नसञ्ज्ञिकम् ।  
गुरुभक्तेश्वरम्मानं गुणातीतमुपास्महे ॥  
  
पूर्वागमेषु बहुशो दीक्षा सन्ति शुभत्मिका ।  
तान् योर्भेदो महसर्वमुपाधिना ॥  
  
उत्तरेष्वागमेषूक्ता मलत्रयविभेदिनी ।  
तस्पापं ह्येकयोर्भेदो नेक्ष्यते निरुपाधिना ॥  
  
आतन्य चिन्मयाभिख्या शिवजीवैक्यकारिणी ।  
तद्विधि वक्ष्य सकलं शिवदेशिकवाक्यतः ॥  
  
अथ यथोक्तलक्षणं आचार्यः उक्तलक्षणम् ।  
  
शिष्यं लब्ध्वा विहितकाले विहितदेशे यागानां द्रव्याण्याहृत्य ।   
मण्टपादिकं यथोक्तलक्षणं विधाय तद्यथा- श्लोकम् -  
  
नवपञ्चत्रिहस्तैर्वा मण्टपं कारयेद्गुरुः ।  
नवपञ्च कृतं ज्ञेयं द्वादशस्तम्भसंयुतम् ॥

एतेषां मण्टपानाञ्च पञ्चहस्तोन्नतं विदुः ॥

tatva dīkṣāṃ samākhyātaṃ śivasaṃyogakāriṇī |  
liṃgatvameva iti syādyate deśikana tu |  
ityādyātmākhya dīkṣeyaṃ sākṣāccinmayarūpiṇī |  
svayaṃ paraśiva sākṣātvaliṃgāṃgasvarūpakaḥ |  
bhavediyaṃ parā dīkṣā vyomagrahasamāhūyaḥ |  
  
ayaṃ śrī saccidānandabrahmātmaka itīryate | satyaśuddhākhya dīkṣāyam   
sarvamaṃgalakāriṇī | iti evaṃkṛta vedādīkṣāyā gurusvabhūme   
sarvatatvoparisthitaśivaśāṃbhavīṃ vibhāvya tadanantaraṃ heṣiṣya taddarśanena   
iṣṭaliṃgātparaṃ vastu tvayānājñāta svaṃ bho śiṣyaśarīrapādaparyantaṃ   
pratāninayañca | śiṣyaliṃgapatiḥ satitvamiti bhāvena kāyakāraṇa   
bhāvārcaṇamiṣṭādidiṣu kuru | bho śiṣya niruparādhi prāṇipathanakuru | he śiṣya   
  
p. 40)  
  
ādimadhyāntarahitaparaśivaḥ sohamiti niṣṭhāṃ kuru | he śiṣya asmin liṃge   
bhāvalayaṃ kuru | bho śiṣya etatsamastaṃ samyak jñātvā nivedya ca vigata   
saṃśayaḥ san saṃpavartaya ca | ityevamekāgracittādi saptadīkṣābhirupadiśya |   
prāṇeṣṭataṃ manasa iti śivena vacasātveti mantrāṃ paṭhitvā | prāṇeṣvaṃtaṃ   
manaso liṃgamāhūyasmin krodho yā ca tṛṣṭā kṣamā ca | tṛṣṭāṃ citvā hetu   
jālasya mūlaṃ budhvā | sañcintyasthāpayitvā | dharudre rudra ekṛtvamāhuḥ |   
rudraṃ śāśvitaṃ vai purāṇaṃ śive na vacasātvā giriśācchāvathāmasi |   
yathānassarvamijjagadapakṣbhāgaṃ sumanā asat | tataḥ śiṣya dakṣiṇakarṇe   
praṇavapañcākṣarīṃ trivāraṃ śanairupadiśya | uktañca |  
  
iṣṭaliṃgātparaṃ vastu nājñātaṃ ekāgradīkṣeya bhavanāgaśoṣiṇī |   
śarīrapādaparyantaṃ na muñcati pratāṃnyataḥ dṛḍhapratākhya vīkṣeya   
liṃgāṣārābhimāninī liṃgapatīḥ satī cāhamiti bhāvena sarvadā kāyasya   
karuṇānāñca bhāvasya ca samarpaṇam |   
iṣṭādiṣu ca kurvīta liṃgeṣu vigataspṛhaḥ |   
pañcendriyārcanābhikhyā dīkṣānāmanuyogataḥ |  
niraparādhiprāṇivadaḥ | kriyate namyasūyayā |  
ahiṃsākhyamimāṃ prāhurācārya śivaśāstriṇaḥ |  
ādimadhyāvasānena nahitaḥ paramaḥ śivaḥ |  
  
p. 41)  
  
sohamityādikā niṣṭhā hyeṣāliṃganijākarā |  
tasmin liṃge bhāvalayaḥ sā dīkṣā tu mano layā |  
etatsamastaṃ nibadhya | jñātvā vigatasaṃśayaḥ |  
vartateśśaraṇaṃ sadyo buddhidīkṣāmahottareti |  
  
evaṃ kṛtamanudīkṣāyā guru svabhāvena pūrvoktabhāvīkaṃ nā manasā   
saṃyojya dṛṣṭavāniyatayāṣṭāa vāmakaratala vihitāliṃgetāṃ   
kalāmūlamantreṇa saṃsthāpya | uktañca -  
  
gurumūrtiṃ samādāya śiṣyamastakake kalām |  
nijasadbhāvamātreṇa nīlokyā kṛsṛtāṃ kalām |  
yathā kalā tathā bhāvo yathā bhāvastathā manaḥ |  
yathā manastadā dṛṣṭiḥ yathā dṛṣṭistathā sthalam |  
jalakuṃbhāgrasadbhaktā tailaṃ binduryayā tathā |  
dehaṃprāṇātmano vyāptvā saṃsthitā śāṃbhavīṃ parā |  
jvalatkālānalābhāsa ravikoṭi samaprabhā |  
tasyordhve tu śikhāntaṃ vai cidrūpaṃ paramākalā |  
nākāśaparamā sūkṣma tatvānāṃ bodhinī parā |  
tāmākṛṣya yathānyāyaṃ liṃge samupaveśayediti |   
  
asmin liṃge sa sthiro bhaveti devaṃ vijñāpya | he paraśiva carācarātmako na bhava   
bhaktaikta tanurbhatyupapadiśya he śiṣya bhūtiprāṇina bhavaliṃgaprāṇi   
bhavatyupadiśya tataḥ pādodakaprasādau datvā guruliṃgajaṃgamān   
  
p. 42)  
  
sampagarcayeti vinītaṃ śiṣyaṃ pratyuktvā |  
āṣṭhāvaraṇa sarvathā śraddhāṃ kuru pañcācāreṣu pravartaye | uktaṃ ca -   
  
prādodaka prasādaṃ ca guruliṃgañca jaṃgamān |  
samyagarcayeti vinītaṃ śiṣyaṃ pratyuktvā | aṣṭāvaraṇe sarvathā śraddhāṃ kuru |   
pañcācāryeṣu pravartaya |  
uktañca -   
  
pādodakaṃ prasādaṃ ca guruliṃgaṃ ca jaṃgaman |  
mantrarudrākṣabhasmāni cāṣṭāvaraṇasaṃjñikam |  
liṃgācāraḥ sadācāraḥ śivācārastathaiva ca |  
gurubhṛtyā cakāraṃ ca pañcācāra iti smṛtāḥ |  
  
tataḥ śobhanakāśeyādi vastreṇa liṃgaṃ budhvā | śiṣyassakaṇṭhenamo brahmaṇe   
dhāraṇayiti mantreṇa vṛttena brahmāgraṃ dhikkṛtvā namo brahmaṇe   
dhāraṇaṃ me astu nirākaraṇaṃ dhārayitā bhūyāsaṃ karṇayośśataṃ   
māccodhyam | mamāmuṣya om | tatra samayācārādikaṃ bodhayet | asya   
dṛṣṭipātaṃ yattaddravyaṃ parivarjayet | śuddhadravyaṃ samāsāśca   
liṃgānāntu samarpayet | iti vratasamāyukto vīraśaivo śiṣyate |   
vīramāhecvarāṇāṃ ca sāmānya vratamuṃcate | pūrvoktaśivamudrāsu taddārīṣu   
ca sarvataḥ | arthataḥ prāṇataḥ prājña bhaktiḥ kuryāt vṛthāmapi | pāpaṃ teṣu   
na kartavyaṃ svayaṃmeda kadācana |  
  
p. 43)  
  
kṛtaṃ vareṇa cetpāpaṃ nopekṣetkaraṇai stribhiḥ |  
balakānakṣaye tatra durbalaścedvrajedyataḥ |  
karaṇaistu vihīnaścedbuddhiṃ tatra na nikṣipet |  
dvikaroti yathā liṃga guruṇādīkṣayā dhanā |  
tathā prabhṛtiliṃgaikyamavinā bhavayogataḥ |  
dehaprāṇena saṃsthāpya prāṇaliṃge ca nikṣipet |  
etābhyāṃ prāṇadehābhyāṃ pṛthakbhāva na kārayet |  
guruṇā dattaliṃgantu sāvadhānena dhārayet |  
pramādātpatite liṃgaṃ prāṇānapi parityajet |  
patite prāṇaliṃge yaḥ prāṇān dhatte narādhamaḥ |  
ca caṇḍāla iti jñeyaḥ śivadrohī na ta. spṛśet |  
prāṇaliṃge vrate lupte hastakhaḍgādapaṃcayet |  
mukhaṃ paṃkajavacchidyādvibhṛtyassalakṣaṇam |  
vīraśaiva kramaproktaḥ prāyaścittavivarjitaḥ |  
  
ityājñārūpeṇopadaśya ekakālaṃ dvikālaṃ trikālaṃ vā sveṣṭaliṃgaṃ   
samarcayet | dupadiśya | uktaṃ ca ekakālaṃ dvikālaṃ vā trikālaṃ vā samarcayediti   
|  
atha śiṣyo gurūṇāṃ ca praṇavamantra yamācaret |  
saṃpūjya deśikāyaṃ dakṣiṇāṃ dāpayettataḥ | tva   
gobhūmi dātiśca vahopakaraṇāni ca |  
ācāryāya pradātavyaṃ śraddhā bhaktipurassaram |  
ṛtvikjñānānāṃ sarveṣāṃ dakṣiṇāṃ dāpayettataḥ |  
bhojayecchivabhaktāśca tāmbūlāni samarcayet |  
  
p. 44)  
  
gurupūjāṃ ca kartavyaṃ bhojayettadanantaram |  
dīkṣāvidhirayaṃ proktaṃ sarvatatvātmakaḥ paraḥ |  
jeyasu śivarātre mahākāle liṃgodbhavatu me sadā |  
rātrau dvisaptaghaṭikā śivasaṃpūrcaṃdrike |  
kṣīṇacandro na likhyete sudhiyo śṛṇu ṣaṇmukhā |  
tatrāpi śivarātrau ca saṃkrānte pañcaparvasu |  
tithinakṣatravārādi sarvadoṣā parityajet |  
devatā sthāpanaṃ kṛtvā dhāraṇaṃ copadeśikā |  
vratamācaraṇe vatsaḥ samāsācchṛṇu ṣaṇmukhāḥ |  
nāmakriyā guṇātītā saguṇai guṇai saha |  
sadāśivaṃ mahādevaṃ mahāpātakanāśanam |  
kena doṣo bhagavate sarve vai devatādhipāḥ ||   
tatotsave tu kāle tu vāranakṣatra dūṣake |  
etāṃ satsaktavidhinā śivarātre ca sarvasu |  
eteṣu parvakāleṣu pratiṣṭhā mācaretsudhī |  
nahi doṣā ca he devi dharmasyaitā mahāsmṛtā |  
  
|| sāmbaśivāya namaḥ ||
